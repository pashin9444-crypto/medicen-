How they're made

Six steps. Six months.

Living Terrain extracts aren't rushed. Mushrooms and botanicals — many grown and gathered fresh — are drawn through a deliberate, multi-stage extraction that unlocks the full spectrum of beneficial compounds: beta-glucans from the mushrooms, polyphenols and adaptogens from the herbs.

The result is a full-spectrum extract that captures both water- and alcohol-soluble constituents, made in small batches with nothing artificial added.

"Maximum extraction. Maximum goodness."

Heritage

From hand-labeled beginnings

Living Terrain began as small, hand-labeled, spore-to-bottle blends — made one batch at a time. That same care carries into the clinician-crafted line today.

What started as a personal practice grew into a considered collection: the same fresh botanicals, the same slow extraction, now formulated with the rigor of a Physician Assistant's training.
