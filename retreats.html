Hands-on craft

Learn to Make Mushroom Extracts

Led by Samantha Maisel, MD, and Serena Ling, PA-C, this hands-on workshop walks you through the dual-extraction method — the same process behind Living Terrain — using Reishi, Lion's Mane, and Turkey Tail. You'll understand why both water and alcohol extraction matter, and leave with extracts you made yourself.

Daily practice

Stretching & Routines for Longevity

Longevity is built in the small, repeatable habits of each day. Through gentle stretching, centering breath, and simple restorative routines, you'll practice a rhythm you can carry home — one that supports mobility, calm, and resilience for the long term.

Setting

Immersive Nature

Each retreat is set in the living landscapes of Northern California — from alpine Lake Tahoe to the coastal light of Santa Cruz and the rolling hills of Sonoma. Time outdoors, water therapy, and mindful walks reconnect you to the terrain that sustains you.
